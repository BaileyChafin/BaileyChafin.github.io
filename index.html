Why I Chose NKU

I chose to attend Northern Kentucky University because of their class options. The university has so many unique options for majors and minors compared to other in-state universities. For one thing, NKU has an animation course that fits what I need better than any other university. The 3DVFX major and the Criminalistics minor will be beneficial to me because I want to be a Forensic Animator after graduation. In that field I would need to be able to digitally recreate a crime scene that can be presented to a jury in court.
