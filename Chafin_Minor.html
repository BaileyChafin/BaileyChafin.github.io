Criminalistics

The Criminalistics minor at NKU focuses on how science is utilized in the criminal justice system. This means that students learn about how to collect and analyze evidence, and how it is presented during trial. I am currently taking two classes for my minor. The classes are really interesting because I am getting a new look into the criminal justice system, which I had not been previously exposed. Next semester, I have the chance to take my first evidence analysis collection and analysis class. I really look forward to it because I think my work as a forensic animator will be able to help many people! To learn more about the Criminalistics minor, go to NKU's website!
